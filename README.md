# ⚖️ Law Office Landing Page

Modern, premium landing page for a Serbian law office focused on business law and legal support for companies.

This project is designed to demonstrate a clean, professional, and high-converting landing page tailored for legal services.

---

## 🚀 Overview

The goal of this project is to present a law office in a serious, trustworthy, and elegant way — avoiding generic templates and focusing on clarity, structure, and client conversion.

Target audience:
- Small and medium businesses
- Entrepreneurs
- Company owners
- Clients in need of legal certainty and ongoing support

---

## ✨ Features

- Premium and minimal design
- Fully responsive layout
- Clear content structure
- Focus on conversion (contact & inquiry)
- Professional Serbian copy (no lorem ipsum)
- Service-oriented sections
- Simple and elegant UI

---

## 📄 Sections Included

- Hero section (main message + CTA)
- Trust & credibility
- Services overview
- Why choose us
- Process (how it works)
- Contact form
- Final call to action
